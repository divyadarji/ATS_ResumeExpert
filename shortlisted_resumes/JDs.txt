## QA Tester (3+ Years Experience)

About the Role:

We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced QA Tester to join our dynamic team. As a QA Tester, you will play a critical role in ensuring the quality and reliability of our software products. You will be responsible for designing, developing, and executing test plans and test cases, identifying and documenting defects, and collaborating with developers and product owners to ensure high-quality releases.

Responsibilities:

   Develop and execute comprehensive test plans and test cases based on software requirements and design specifications.
   Perform various testing types, including functional, regression, integration, performance, and user acceptance testing.
   Identify, document, and track software defects using bug tracking systems.
   Collaborate closely with developers and product owners to clarify requirements, troubleshoot issues, and ensure timely resolution of defects.
   Participate in sprint planning, daily stand-ups, and retrospective meetings.
   Contribute to the continuous improvement of QA processes and methodologies.
   Analyze test results and provide clear and concise reports on software quality.
   Automate test cases where appropriate using relevant testing tools and frameworks.
   Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in software testing.
   Maintain a thorough understanding of the software development lifecycle (SDLC).

Qualifications:

   Minimum of 3 years of experience in software quality assurance testing.
   Strong understanding of software testing methodologies, principles, and techniques.
   Experience in developing and executing test plans, test cases, and test scripts.
   Proficiency in using bug tracking systems (e.g., Jira, Bugzilla).
   Experience with various testing types, including functional, regression, integration, performance, and user acceptance testing.
   Familiarity with test automation tools and frameworks (e.g., Selenium, Appium, JUnit, TestNG).
   Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
   Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
   Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
   Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field is preferred.

Bonus Points:

   Experience with Agile development methodologies (e.g., Scrum, Kanban).
   Experience with API testing.
   Experience with database testing.
   Certification in software testing (e.g., ISTQB).
